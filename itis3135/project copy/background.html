What About Collin's Cheesecake Factory?



Who started the business?


The person who started the business is named Nicketa Collins. She is the owner of Collin's Cheesecake Factory. Xavier Collins, is the co-owner of the business as well. The two came up with the idea of opening a cheesecake business by them always going out to treat themselves to cheesecake at the Cheesecake Factory. So They decided to open up thier own place and thats how it began. Both Nicketa and Xavier have masters degree in entrepreneurship, so they are very capable of running a business. The business has been up for about 4 years and is making a good reputation in the Charlotte area.


What Makes this Place So Popular?


What makes this place so popular is it's various amounts of cheesecakes. Here in Charlotte we have been rated only 4.5 to 5 stars ever since we first opened up. There are over 30 types of different flavored cheesecakes at our shop. We think about it, we have it! Furthermore, we also offer cheesecake baking classes. This will allow people to learn how to bake cheesecakes and it will bring more clients to our factory.
